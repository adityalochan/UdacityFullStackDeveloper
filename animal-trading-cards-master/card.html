CHEETAH

Cheetahs are worlds fastest land mammal...

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Average Length3.6 - 4.9 ft
  • Average Lifespan12 years
  • HabitatSahara Dessert

The cheetah is a lightly built, spotted cat characterised by a small rounded head, a short snout, black tear-like facial streaks, a deep chest, long thin legs
and a long tail. Its slender, canine-like form is highly adapted for speed, and contrasts sharply with the robust build of the genus Panthera.
